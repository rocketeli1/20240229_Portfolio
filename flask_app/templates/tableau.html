Tableau Projects

This page contains a few of the projects that I have built using Tableau. I completed the Tableau A-Z: Hands-On Tableau Training for Data Science course that is offered through Udemy in February of this year. Throughout my journey, I have gained comprehensive skills in Tableau, empowering me to effectively visualize and analyze data. I began by learning how to connect various datasets, including Excel and CSV files. As I progressed, I honed my ability to create diverse visualizations such as bar charts, area charts, maps, scatterplots, pie charts, and tree maps. Additionally, I developed proficiency in designing interactive dashboards and captivating storylines, enabling me to present visual data insights with accessibility and clarity. The course covered essential concepts such as the different types of joins, data blending, table calculations, parameters, and working with timeseries data. I also delved into advanced topics such as aggregation, granularity, and level of detail, alongside mastering techniques for data preparation and export.

By the course's end, I emerged as a highly skilled Tableau user, capable of extracting valuable insights and creating compelling visualizations for diverse applications, from analyzing customer purchase behavior to forecasting sales trends and production bottlenecks. Whether starting from scratch or aiming to enhance existing Tableau skills, this course provided me with a flexible and comprehensive learning experience tailored to my skill development needs.
